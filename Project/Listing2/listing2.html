This spacious and brand new two-storey suite at Riverside Square has everything you could want in a luxury suite. It’s 1,550 square feet of perfection with a 330 square foot terrace (larger than most Toronto backyards) and gas BBQ connection.

The main floor wows the moment you walk through the front door. This large open-concept space is perfect for entertaining. Natural light pours in from the floor to ceiling south-facing windows that lead out to your large terrace. A very generous sized main floor bedroom or office offers ample storage and is adjacent to a two piece bathroom.

Inside, 9 foot exposed concrete ceilings, engineered hardwood flooring, floor to ceiling windows on each floor. On the second floor you’ll find a master retreat with soaring ceilings, large ensuite bathroom, much closet space and walk-out to expansive balcony with spectacular views. A spa-inspired bathroom meets the second bedroom where you’ll find those same impressive views and a generous walk-in closet. Two parking and locker included.

Neighbourhood - Leslieville

As you cross over the Don Valley River through Riverside, you’re transported to the highly sought after neighbourhood of Leslieville. This Queen Street East stretch is known for its boutique shopping, incredible restaurants, and growing number of micro-breweries to please. Home to the iconic Broadview Hotel which offers three unique experiences inside and breathtaking views. Just to the north along Gerrard Street East you'll find the new it spot for Toronto foodies looking for the next best dish. A number of recreational outlets and parks are offered throughout the neighbourhood as well including Jimmie Simpson Rec Centre and Park, Riverdale Park and Withrow Park to name a few. Leslieville has earned its reputation as a family-friendly neighbourhood, a place where young professionals and young families can enjoy the community and still live a full urban lifestyle.
